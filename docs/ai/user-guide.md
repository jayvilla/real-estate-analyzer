# AI Features User Guide

## Overview

The Real Estate Analyzer platform includes powerful AI-powered features to help you analyze properties, evaluate deals, and gain insights into your portfolio. This guide will help you get the most out of these features.

## Getting Started

### Accessing AI Features

1. **Navigate to AI Insights**: Click on "AI Insights" in the main navigation
2. **Select a Property or Deal**: Choose the property or deal you want to analyze
3. **Choose an AI Feature**: Select from the available AI-powered analyses

## Available Features

### 1. Property Analysis

**What it does**: Provides comprehensive AI-powered analysis of a property's investment potential.

**How to use**:
1. Go to Properties page
2. Select a property
3. Click "AI Analysis" button
4. Review the analysis results

**What you'll get**:
- Property strengths and weaknesses
- Investment potential rating (high/medium/low)
- Recommendations for improvement
- Risk factors
- Market positioning analysis

**Best for**: Evaluating new properties, identifying improvement opportunities

### 2. Deal Recommendation

**What it does**: AI-powered recommendation on whether to proceed with a deal.

**How to use**:
1. Go to Deals page
2. Select a deal
3. Click "Get AI Recommendation"
4. Review the recommendation

**What you'll get**:
- Overall recommendation (buy/pass/negotiate)
- Confidence level
- Key factors influencing the decision
- Suggested negotiation points
- Expected returns analysis

**Best for**: Making investment decisions, deal evaluation

### 3. Risk Assessment

**What it does**: Comprehensive risk analysis for properties or deals.

**How to use**:
1. Select a property or deal
2. Click "Assess Risk"
3. Review risk factors and mitigation strategies

**What you'll get**:
- Overall risk rating
- Financial risk factors
- Market risk factors
- Location-based risks
- Risk mitigation strategies

**Best for**: Due diligence, portfolio risk management

### 4. Natural Language Queries

**What it does**: Ask questions about your portfolio in plain English.

**How to use**:
1. Go to AI Insights â†’ Chat tab
2. Type your question in natural language
3. Review the results

**Example queries**:
- "Show me all properties in California"
- "What's my average cap rate?"
- "List properties with cash-on-cash return above 10%"
- "Compare properties by ROI"
- "What's my total portfolio value?"

**What you'll get**:
- Structured query results
- Formatted, human-readable output
- Query history for easy re-runs

**Best for**: Quick data queries, portfolio exploration

### 5. Portfolio Insights

**What it does**: AI-generated insights about your entire portfolio.

**How to use**:
1. Go to AI Insights â†’ Insights tab
2. Select "Portfolio Insights"
3. Review the insights

**What you'll get**:
- Portfolio performance analysis
- Diversification assessment
- Growth opportunities
- Risk concentration analysis
- Optimization recommendations

**Best for**: Portfolio management, strategic planning

### 6. AI-Generated Summaries

**What it does**: Creates comprehensive summaries of properties, deals, markets, or your portfolio.

**How to use**:
1. Navigate to the item you want summarized
2. Click "Generate Summary"
3. Choose format (JSON, PDF, HTML, Markdown)
4. Select language
5. Download or email the summary

**Summary types**:
- **Portfolio Summary**: Overview of entire portfolio
- **Property Summary**: Individual property performance
- **Deal Summary**: Deal analysis and recommendations
- **Market Report**: Market conditions for a location
- **Executive Summary**: High-level dashboard summary

**Best for**: Reporting, presentations, documentation

### 7. Investment Strategy Suggestions

**What it does**: Provides personalized investment strategy recommendations.

**How to use**:
1. Go to AI Insights
2. Click "Investment Strategy"
3. Review strategy recommendations

**What you'll get**:
- Portfolio optimization strategies
- Growth opportunities
- Risk management strategies
- Market entry/exit recommendations

**Best for**: Strategic planning, portfolio optimization

### 8. Market Commentary

**What it does**: AI-generated commentary on current market conditions.

**How to use**:
1. Go to AI Insights
2. Click "Market Commentary"
3. Optionally specify a zip code for location-specific commentary

**What you'll get**:
- Market trends and patterns
- Price movements
- Inventory levels

- Regional insights

**Best for**: Market research, investment timing

## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Provide Complete Information

The more complete your property or deal data, the better the AI analysis:
- Fill in all property details
- Include financial assumptions
- Add market data when available

### 2. Use Natural Language Queries Effectively

- Be specific: "Properties in Los Angeles with cap rate above 8%"
- Use filters: "Deals from last month"
- Ask for comparisons: "Compare properties by ROI"
- Request calculations: "What's my total portfolio value?"

### 3. Review and Refine

- AI recommendations are suggestions, not guarantees
- Always review analysis results
- Use insights to inform decisions, not replace judgment
- Combine AI insights with your expertise

### 4. Leverage Caching

- Repeated queries are cached for faster results
- Similar properties/deals may use cached insights
- Cache reduces costs and improves performance

### 5. Customize Preferences

Go to AI Insights â†’ Preferences to:
- Set preferred AI provider
- Choose default language
- Enable/disable auto-generate summaries
- Configure notifications

## Understanding AI Responses

### Confidence Levels

AI recommendations include confidence levels:
- **High (80-100%)**: Strong recommendation with high certainty
- **Medium (50-79%)**: Moderate recommendation with some uncertainty
- **Low (<50%)**: Weak recommendation, consider additional analysis

### Risk Ratings

Risk assessments use standard ratings:
- **Low**: Minimal risk factors
- **Medium**: Some risk factors present
- **High**: Significant risk factors
- **Critical**: Major risk factors requiring attention

### Investment Potential

Property analysis includes investment potential:
- **High**: Strong investment opportunity
- **Medium**: Moderate investment opportunity
- **Low**: Weak investment opportunity

## Troubleshooting

### AI Feature Not Available

- Check if feature flag is enabled for your organization
- Verify you have proper permissions
- Contact administrator if issues persist

### Slow Response Times

- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few moments (may be rate limited)
- Use cached results when available

### Unexpected Results

- Verify property/deal data is complete
- Try re-running the analysis
- Check if market data is up to date
- Contact support if issues persist

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How accurate are AI recommendations?**
A: AI recommendations are based on data analysis and patterns, but should be used as one input for decision-making, not the sole factor.

**Q: Can I customize AI prompts?**
A: Currently, prompts are optimized by the system. Customization may be available in future updates.

**Q: How often is AI analysis updated?**
A: Analysis is generated on-demand. Cached results are refreshed based on data changes.

**Q: What AI models are used?**
A: The platform supports multiple providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama). Your organization's preferred provider is used.

**Q: Are there usage limits?**
A: Rate limits are configured per organization. Contact your administrator for details.
